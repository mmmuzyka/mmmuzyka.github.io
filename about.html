About me

My name is Mark and I have been practicing tutoring English for 6 years. I have tried myself first as an English tutor when I was 15. I was scared and inexperienced, which I cannot say about myself now. I have helped more than 20 young learners to feel freer in expressing themselves in English, and I am extremely proud of them and myself.

Besides my tutoring experience, I study English and Literature at LCC International University, which is American-styled, where the language of instruction is English. I have taken an Academic and Creative Writing course, Public Speaking, Linguistics, and TESOL (Teaching English as a second language.) I have also passed TOEFL.

I prepare materials by myself and try to organize my lessons to be as creative as possible in a playful way. I also try to be not only a teacher for my students but also a friend, which is I guess a perfect strategy to break a speaking barrier. The last but not least thing which is extremely important for my students is that there is NO HOMEWORK in my classes.
