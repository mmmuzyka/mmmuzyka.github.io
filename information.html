Information about lessons

A trial lesson is for free. Lessons are individual. For students younger than 12, a lesson lasts for 40 minutes. For students older than 12, a lesson lasts for 1 hour. Classes are held at least 2 times per week, or more. There is no point in having a class only once per week. If there is a situation that a student or I cannot be present in class, it should be told in advance (at least in 12 hours) Materials needed for a class: only paper and markers.
